Our Story

With a deep appreciation for turning on snow and innovative shape design, we create truly unique riding experiences, simply for the sake of beauty and joy.

「BlackHawk」は友人たちの集いから生まれました。

In Japan in 2021 we witnessed the country's thriving snow-surf culture and their specifically “built for deep powder” boards. This spoke to our individual riding styles and interests. So when the opportunity arose to start our own brand, it was clear that the focus would lie on making boards that were easy-to-ride in these mountains. At the core of every BlackHawk board, is a sidecut that allows you to carve like no others on hard groomers, and a shape that lets you float through powder with ease.

Our boards aim to eliminate any barriers between the rider and actualize their dream.

A lot more than the obvious goes into every board we build. We believe that anything is possible. Each and every board is made by a highly skilled, magical, hands-on craftsmen, who is more at home on a snowboard than their own feet. Our snowboards are for the world’s best athletes and the worlds best terrain. We use the most exotic, high performance, environmentally friendly materials as well as the safest, most efficient, environmentally friendly processes available.

We are making a board for the future.

We are completely committed to our team and snowboarders everywhere and will push progression beyond industry standard. Each board is handbuilt in Japan our secret factory, high in the mountains. We know that once you ride a BlackHawk you will never want to ride anything else again. Come and ride a BlackHawk with us.
