Access

The store is located in the Rusutsu Resort just a few minutes from the slopes, and 1km from Rusutsu town.

From Sapporo

[By Car]

Follow National Highway 230 South from Sapporo. Approx. 1h 43min.

[By Bus]

Take the Donan bus from in front of Sapporo Station to Rusutsu Kougen. Approx. 2hrs. Price is around 1,200 ~ 2,100¥.

From Chitose / New Chitose Airport

[By Car]

Take National Highway 16 West from Chitose for approx. 21km then turn right onto National Highway 453. Follow the road for 34km and then continue straight onto National Highway 276 for another 19km, then turn left onto National Highway 230 for 5km. The resort and our store will be on your right. Approx. 1hr 38min.

[By Bus]

An Aiport Limosine bus is also available. Please ask at the airport information desk for schedule and pricing details.
