Snowsurf Camber

The flagship and the original BlackHawk shape. This board has remained relatively unchanged since the 80's, why change a good thing? The name U Series comes from the convex shape of the nose, optimizing natural flow and maneuverability while creating more float in soft snow. The progressive sidecut provides an extra acceleration and response as well as stability, enabling smoother turn initiation and exit in all snow conditions. The rockered diamond tail holds an aggressive edge in deep bottom turns but can also produce looseness when unweighting the tail in top turns. Envision the turns you want and the U Series will make it happen.
